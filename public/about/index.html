About

I’m Aporv. 🦊

// info

I’m a 16-year-old currently training to be an electronics technician. When I’m not studying how circuits should work, I’m usually busy breaking and fixing things that probably belonged in a museum or a landfill ten years ago.

// what I Do

I have a deep-seated love for fucking around with old hardware and software. There’s something deeply satisfying about making a piece of “obsolete” tech do something it was never intended to do.

// why

Most of the shit I make is completely useless. I don’t build things because there’s a market for them or because they solve a “problem.” I build them because:

  1. I thought of it at 2 AM.
  2. I convinced myself it would be a “productive use of time.”
  3. I wanted to see if I could.

// interests & identity

  • Furry: I’m a fox. Usually found lurking in the tech side of the fandom.
  • Hardware: Still learning, but usually found yelling at microcontrollers.
  • Software: Writing things either because I’m pissed, or because I feel like others might find it funny (weird)
